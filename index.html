Prices are entered by you — update the current price whenever you want fresh numbers. There's no live market feed (that would need a paid data provider), but you can update everything in seconds.

Paste a bank / UPI SMS

Copy the alert from your messages and paste it. Ledger reads the amount and whether it's money in or out, then you confirm.

Upload statement (CSV)

Export your account statement as CSV from your bank's site, then upload it. Ledger auto-detects date, description, and debit/credit columns.

About automatic / invoice tracking: A website can't silently read your phone's SMS or open PDF invoices on its own — your browser blocks that for privacy. The realistic, private workflow is the one above: paste a message or upload a CSV, and entries are created for you in bulk. For PDF invoices, copy the total into the SMS box or add it manually. Everything stays on your device.
